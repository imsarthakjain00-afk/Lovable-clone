# Website Design Catalog

## DESIGN_ID: EDITORIAL_MONOCHROME

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Editorial Monochrome
**DESCRIPTION**: High-contrast editorial layouts combining oversized serif typography, monochrome imagery, strict grids, and restrained interface controls.
**BEST_FOR**: Architecture, Fashion, Creative studios, Photography, Art portfolios, Premium agencies

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Editorial, Intellectual, Premium, Restrained

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Asymmetric editorial grid. Large image regions. Strong typographic hierarchy. Minimal decorative UI.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Black, charcoal, warm white, muted grey. (e.g. bg-stone-50, bg-zinc-950)

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: High-contrast serif display typography (e.g., 'Playfair Display' or 'Merriweather' via Google Fonts) paired with compact neutral sans-serif metadata (e.g., 'Inter').

**HEADING_STYLE**: Oversized, bold, serif.
**BODY_STYLE**: Compact, readable sans-serif, slightly muted (opacity-80).

**SPACING_DENSITY**: High whitespace, dramatic margins.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Thin solid borders (1px) in muted colors where necessary to delineate sections.
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Minimal to none. 0px to 4px max.

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Compact rectangular controls. Subtle borders. No pill-shaped CTAs. Strong hover states with invert colors.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Flat. No drop shadows. Just borders or distinct background colors.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Minimal top bar or stark side navigation.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Full height image with overlaid serif text or split 50/50 image and text.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Desaturated or monochrome imagery preferred.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Slow image reveals. Subtle opacity transitions. No excessive spring animation.

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Understated hover states (opacity changes or simple underlines).

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Preserve editorial hierarchy. Stack asymmetric grids intentionally. Maintain oversized serif headings with responsive clamp sizing.

**AVOID**: Purple gradients. Glassmorphism. Large pill buttons. Generic SaaS cards. Bright primary colors.

## DESIGN_ID: MINIMAL_LUXURY

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Minimal Luxury
**DESCRIPTION**: An ultra-refined, understated premium presentation utilizing abundant whitespace, elegant sans-serifs, and warm earthy or neutral tones.
**BEST_FOR**: Luxury brands, Boutique hotels, High-end skincare, Fine jewelry, Premium personal portfolios.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Refined, Exclusive, Calm, Understated.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Extreme restraint. Centralized alignment or symmetrical grids. Floating elements.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Warm neutrals (cream, beige, soft taupe, muted gold). 

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Elegant, geometric sans-serif (e.g., 'Outfit' or 'Jost'). Light or regular weights for headings.

**HEADING_STYLE**: Wide letter-spacing (tracking), all-caps for smaller headings, thin weights for main headings.
**BODY_STYLE**: Generous line-height, elegant readable sans-serif.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Very high. Maximum whitespace.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Barely visible separators.
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Subtle rounding (4px to 8px).

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Pill-shaped or soft-rounded buttons. Ghost buttons with delicate borders are common.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Very subtle, diffused shadows. Elements should feel like they are lightly resting on the page.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Centered logo, minimal links, lots of breathing room.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Sparse. Often just a beautiful background image with a centered delicate title.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: High-quality, warm, soft-lit photography.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Gentle fades. Elements fading up gently on scroll. 

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Smooth, extended duration transitions.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Ensure text doesn't become too small. Keep spacing generous even on small screens.

**AVOID**: High-contrast neon colors. Chunky borders. Dense information packing. Aggressive animations.

## DESIGN_ID: BRUTALIST_ARCHITECTURAL

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Brutalist Architectural
**DESCRIPTION**: Structural grids, raw typography, bold borders, and an experimental, unpolished aesthetic.
**BEST_FOR**: Experimental design studios, Art galleries, Underground music, Tech-forward architecture, Streetwear.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Raw, Unapologetic, Bold, Experimental.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Visible grid lines, overlapping elements, deliberate misalignment, stark contrast.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Harsh monochrome (pure black and white) with one shocking accent color (neon green, bright red, or electric blue).

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Monospace or extremely bold grotesque sans-serifs (e.g., 'Space Mono', 'Syne', or heavily weighted 'Inter').

**HEADING_STYLE**: Massive, tightly packed, sometimes overflowing the container.
**BODY_STYLE**: Monospace or dense sans-serif.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Can be dense. Elements packed tightly against thick borders.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Thick, stark borders (2px to 4px) delineating all major structural elements.
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Absolutely none. 0px. Sharp corners.

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Large, blocky, high-contrast. Bold borders. Harsh hover color swaps.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Thick borders, no shadows, or harsh solid-color drop shadows (e.g. `box-shadow: 6px 6px 0px #000`).

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Utilitarian, ticker-tape styles, or massive dropdowns.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Typography heavy. Giant marquee text. 

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Unfiltered, raw, sometimes distorted or grayscaled with high contrast.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Abrupt, fast, snappy transitions. No easing. 

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Harsh, immediate changes. 

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Let elements stack with thick borders separating them. Marquees work well.

**AVOID**: Soft gradients. Drop shadows. Rounded corners. Delicate typography.

## DESIGN_ID: MODERN_SAAS

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Modern SaaS
**DESCRIPTION**: Friendly, accessible, trust-building layouts with clear hierarchies, soft corners, and vibrant accents.
**BEST_FOR**: Software products, B2B services, Productivity tools, Dashboards.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Trustworthy, Friendly, Capable, Clear.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Z-pattern reading, clear feature grids, distinct pricing tables, obvious CTAs.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Clean white/light gray backgrounds with vibrant primary colors (e.g. Blue-600, Indigo-600).

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Friendly, legible sans-serif (e.g., 'Inter', 'Roboto').

**HEADING_STYLE**: Bold, tightly kerned.
**BODY_STYLE**: Highly readable, gray (text-gray-600) for lower contrast.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Balanced. 

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Subtle light gray borders (border-gray-200).
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Medium to heavy rounding (8px to 16px).

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Pill-shaped or well-rounded CTA buttons. Solid primary color.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: White cards on very light gray backgrounds with soft drop shadows (`shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`).

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Standard top nav. Logo left, links center, CTA right.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Two-column (text left, illustration/dashboard mockup right) or centered text above a large app screenshot.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: App mockups, friendly illustrations, diverse team photos.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Standard UI animations. Snappy but smoothed out.

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Clear hover states on buttons and cards.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Standard responsive stacking. Hamburger menu. 

**AVOID**: Completely black backgrounds. Hard edges (0px radius). Overly complex typographic layouts.

## DESIGN_ID: GLASS_TECH

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Glass Tech (Glassmorphism)
**DESCRIPTION**: Futuristic, deep interfaces using heavy blur, transparency, and vibrant glowing gradients behind frosted glass panels.
**BEST_FOR**: Web3, AI startups, Futuristic software, Fintech, Gaming.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Futuristic, Cutting-edge, Deep, Ethereal.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Layered. Elements appear to float in 3D space above a vibrant background.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Very dark backgrounds (slate-950, deep purple) with bright, saturated glowing orbs (magenta, cyan, indigo).

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Crisp sans-serif (e.g., 'Inter', 'Outfit').

**HEADING_STYLE**: White or translucent gradient text.
**BODY_STYLE**: Light gray or semi-transparent white (text-white/70).

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Open, to allow background glows to be seen.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Very thin, semi-transparent white borders (border-white/10).
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Heavy rounding (16px to 24px) for glass panels.

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Bright gradient buttons, or glass buttons with subtle glow.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: `backdrop-blur-lg`, semi-transparent background (bg-white/5 or bg-black/20), inner light borders.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Floating glass header pill.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Centered glowing text over a massive complex abstract gradient or 3D render.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Abstract renders, glowing UI elements.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Smooth, fluid floating animations. 

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Hover effects that increase border opacity or background glow.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Ensure blur isn't too performance-heavy. 

**AVOID**: Flat opaque colors. Standard white backgrounds. Hard borders.

## DESIGN_ID: BOLD_CREATIVE

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Bold Creative
**DESCRIPTION**: Vibrant, playful, high-energy layouts with massive typography, bright colors, and unexpected placements.
**BEST_FOR**: Design agencies, Creative portfolios, Event websites, Modern food & beverage brands.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Loud, Energetic, Unconventional, Playful.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Breaking the grid. Overlapping text and images. Dynamic asymmetry.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: High saturation, unexpected pairings (e.g. Electric Pink with Deep Green).

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Massive, quirky display fonts mixed with clean sans-serifs.

**HEADING_STYLE**: Huge, colorful, sometimes rotated or overlapping.
**BODY_STYLE**: Legible but large font size.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Variable. Can be tight in some areas and loose in others.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Thick, colorful borders.
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Either completely sharp or completely pill-shaped. Extreme contrast.

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Large, colorful, bouncy hover effects.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Flat, brightly colored cards with solid black drop shadows.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Oversized menus, sometimes taking up the whole screen when opened.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Massive text filling the screen.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Cut-out elements, playful illustrations, high-contrast photos.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Bouncy, spring-based animations. 

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Very reactive. Hovering elements might rotate or scale significantly.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Large touch targets. Keep the vibrant energy even when stacked.

**AVOID**: Boring grays. Delicate lines. Standard corporate layouts.

## DESIGN_ID: CORPORATE_PREMIUM

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Corporate Premium
**DESCRIPTION**: Professional, highly polished, authoritative designs that instill confidence and scale.
**BEST_FOR**: Enterprise software, Consulting firms, Financial institutions, Large agencies.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Authoritative, Reliable, Global, Polished.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Highly structured, traditional grids (12-column). Predictable and easy to navigate.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Deep navy, slate gray, crisp white, with a singular professional accent (e.g. gold or calm blue).

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Extremely legible, classic sans-serif (e.g., 'Roboto', 'Open Sans').

**HEADING_STYLE**: Moderate weight, clear hierarchy.
**BODY_STYLE**: Standard sizing, highly readable contrast.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Standardized and consistent.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Thin, subtle separators.
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Slight (2px to 4px) to appear modern but serious.

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Rectangular with slight rounding. Solid colors. Clear CTAs.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Clean white cards with very subtle, standard drop shadows.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Comprehensive mega-menus, sticky header.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Professional photography of people/offices or high-quality product renders alongside a strong value proposition.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Authentic corporate photography, high quality, well-lit.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Very minimal. Maybe simple fades on scroll.

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Standard hover color changes.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Standardized, clean collapsing grids.

**AVOID**: Crazy color schemes. Experimental layouts. Hard-to-read typography. Glassmorphism.

## DESIGN_ID: SOFT_MINIMAL

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Soft Minimal
**DESCRIPTION**: Gentle, approachable, and calming designs with soft edges, muted pastels, and a friendly vibe.
**BEST_FOR**: Health & Wellness, Therapy, Sustainable products, Baby brands, Community spaces.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Gentle, Calming, Approachable, Safe.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Symmetrical, floating, open.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Muted pastels (sage green, dusty rose, soft peach, baby blue) and off-whites.

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Rounded sans-serifs (e.g., 'Nunito' or 'Quicksand') or very soft serifs.

**HEADING_STYLE**: Soft, medium weight, approachable.
**BODY_STYLE**: Warm gray text instead of harsh black.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: High. Lots of breathing room.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: Rarely used. If used, very soft and thick (border-4).
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Maximum rounding. Everything is a pill or a circle. (24px to full rounded).

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Fully pill-shaped, soft colors.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Soft, diffused, colored drop shadows.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Simple, rounded floating pill header.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Centered, welcoming text with organic shapes or soft photography.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Soft lighting, natural elements, smiles. Often masked into organic blob shapes or rounded rectangles.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Gentle fading and floating.

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Soft scaling on hover.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Maintain extreme rounded corners and soft colors.

**AVOID**: Sharp edges. Pure black or pure white. High contrast neon colors.

## DESIGN_ID: FUTURISTIC_TECH

**DISPLAY_NAME**: Futuristic Tech (Cyber/Dark Mode)
**DESCRIPTION**: Grid-lines, monospaced accents, terminal-like interfaces, and glowing elements on dark backgrounds.
**BEST_FOR**: Developer tools, Hackathons, Cybersecurity, Tech infrastructure, AI APIs.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Technical, Precision, Advanced, Developer-focused.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Code-editor inspired. Visible grids, technical diagrams.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Deep black/gray background (bg-zinc-950) with neon accents (cyan, green, or orange).

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Clean sans-serif for reading, heavy use of monospace for data and accents.

**HEADING_STYLE**: Bright white, sharp.
**BODY_STYLE**: Light gray. Monospace tags.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Dense but organized, like an IDE.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: 1px solid borders everywhere (border-zinc-800).
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: Minimal (4px max).

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Outlined buttons with glowing hover states, or terminal-style buttons.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: Dark cards with subtle border highlighting.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Dense, technical, top-bar.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Code snippets, terminal windows, or glowing wireframe globe.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: UI diagrams, code blocks, dark-mode dashboards.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Typing effects, blinking cursors, snappy transitions.

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Interactive diagrams, copy-to-clipboard buttons, hover highlights.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: Ensure code blocks can scroll horizontally. 

**AVOID**: Large lifestyle photos. Soft pastels. Serif fonts.

## DESIGN_ID: E_COMMERCE_EDITORIAL

**DISPLAY_NAME**: E-Commerce Editorial
**DESCRIPTION**: A hybrid of minimal editorial presentation with highly optimized shopping patterns. Focuses purely on the product.
**BEST_FOR**: High-end fashion e-commerce, D2C brands, Premium lifestyle products.

**VISUAL_PERSONALITY**: Desirable, Clean, Product-focused.

**LAYOUT_PHILOSOPHY**: Grid-based product galleries. Sticky add-to-cart regions.

**COLOR_STRATEGY**: Pure white or extremely subtle off-white to let product colors pop. Black text.

**TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY**: Very clean, small sans-serif for UI, elegant serif for brand names or large titles.

**HEADING_STYLE**: Elegant, restrained.
**BODY_STYLE**: Small, crisp, functional.

**SPACING_DENSITY**: Very tight in product grids, very loose in lifestyle sections.

**BORDER_STRATEGY**: 1px borders for separating product grid items.
**BORDER_RADIUS_STRATEGY**: 0px. Sharp and clean.

**BUTTON_STRATEGY**: Full-width, solid black "Add to Cart" buttons. No rounding.

**CARD_STRATEGY**: No cards. Products sit directly on the background.

**NAVIGATION_STRATEGY**: Sticky top nav, shopping bag icon prominent.

**HERO_STRATEGY**: Full-screen video or massive high-res lifestyle photo with minimal text.

**IMAGE_STRATEGY**: Extremely high-quality product photography (often cut out on white) and rich lifestyle imagery.

**ANIMATION_STRATEGY**: Subtle image zoom on hover. 

**INTERACTION_STRATEGY**: Quick add-to-cart drawers, image carousels.

**MOBILE_STRATEGY**: 2-column product grids on mobile. Sticky bottom add-to-cart buttons.

**AVOID**: Cluttered backgrounds behind products. Heavy drop shadows. Rounded bubbly UI.
